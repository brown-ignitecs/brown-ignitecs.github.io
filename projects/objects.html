Here are reminders of what we talked about in class:

  • Classes are like collections of information. In this example, we have a class Person, which represents what information we might want to remember about a person
  • Classes have attributes and methods
    • Attributes are pieces of information to remember. Example: you might want to remember what kind of hair someone has
      • this.thing is an attribute
    • Methods are remembering how to do something. Example: you might want to remember how to walk by putting one foot in front of the other
      • thing(){} is a method
  • The constructor is where you say how to create the class (in our example, where we say how we will create a person)
  • To tell the constructor to create a class, we say let person = new Person("name",age,"what they will say", "head type", "eye type", "mouth type", "arm type");
